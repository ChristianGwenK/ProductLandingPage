Apple iPhone (1st generation or Original)

Stereo Headset

A stereo headset is a headset that is capable of playing stereo sound. Some stereo headsets have a microphone and can be used with a cellphone. Stereo sound is basically is sound recorded simultaneously on two different channels, which are then fed to a system capable of differentiating those channels.

Dock connector to USB cable

Used to connect Apple mobile devices like iPhones, iPads, and iPods to host computers, external monitors, cameras, USB battery chargers, and other peripherals.

Cleaning Cloth

The best way to safely clean your electronics is with a slightly moistened microfiber cloth. Microfiber cleaning cloths are soft, washable, reusable, and especially effective in cleaning hard surfaces like your smartphone and oleophobic (oil repellent) iPhone screens.
